Cyber Security for Teen Field Project Report

Conducted at Janta Highschool, Talni

Photos from Janta Highschool, Talni

Team Members

1. Introduction

In today's digital age, technology plays an essential role in our daily lives.

Our project at Janta Highschool, Talni aimed to raise awareness.

2. Motivation

Increasing use of technology and lack of awareness inspired this project.

3. Objectives

  • Educate students on Cyber Security basics.
  • Raise awareness about cyber safety.
  • Teach practical safety measures.
  • Promote responsible use of technology.

4. Methodology

  • Research on cyber threats.
  • Interactive sessions.
  • Distributed handouts.

5. Observations / Data Collected

Students were mostly unaware of phishing and ransomware before the session.

We distributed a survey form to understand their knowledge.

After analysis, awareness improved significantly.

6. Results & Interpretation

85% of students could identify cyber threats after the session.

7. Conclusion

The project successfully raised cyber security awareness.

8. Social Relevance

Cyber education helps create a safe digital society.

9. Challenges Faced

  • Lack of technical knowledge.
  • Limited resources.
  • Low awareness.

10. Suggestions

  • Conduct regular awareness programs.
  • Include cyber security in syllabus.
